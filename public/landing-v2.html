04 · Privacy and Control

Private by default.
Shared only by consent.

Carevo is designed around sourced rules, explicit opt-in sharing, and a clean boundary between AI assistance and human accountability.

No account required

Carevo does not ask for names, member IDs, addresses, or medical record numbers to run the intake flow.

Consent before storage

Conversation sharing appears only after the assessment, stays unchecked by default, and returns a deletion code.

Admin-key protected

Shared logs are confidential review records with deletion handling and minimum-access expectations.

into the right next step.
