04 · Privacy and Control

Private enough to use
real patient data.

Carevo is designed around sourced rules, explicit approvals, and a clean boundary between AI assistance and human accountability.

Source-linked by default

Every care level traces to a specific clinical rule version. No LLM output sits in the routing path — the engine is deterministic.

Human approval at the edge

Authorization, exception handling, and sensitive review can wait for the right person before anything moves.

A record of every action

Every extraction, question, rule match, and recommendation can be traced back to the member case.
